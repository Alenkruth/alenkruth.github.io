About Me

I am currently a first year Ph.D. student at the Computer Engineering Department in the University of Virginia. I am working with Dr. Ashish Venkat on Computer Architecture and Hardware Security.

Between June 2021 and May 2022, I was a CPU Verification Engineer at InCore Semiconductors. I worked on developing UATG, a verification framework for modular RISC-V processors

I graduated with a B.E. in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from Anna University in April 2021. My research interests lie in Computer Architecture, Digital Design and Hardware Security.

Between January 2021 and May 2021, I was a RTL Design Intern at InCore Semiconductors working on my undergraduate thesis. I was involved in the designing of an Execution Unit for the Packed SIMD Extension of RISC-V.

I interned at IIT Palakkad working on Integration of an AES-256 unit into the RI5CY core pipeline(Pulpissimo SoC) with Dr. Satyajit Das.

Most of my past work is open-source, thanks to my organization and collaborators. I love collaborating on open-source projects. Reach out to me!
