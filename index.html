üè∞  Research overview

I work with my students (currently: Jonathan Castello; Tim Goodwin; Nathan Liittschwager; Patrick Redmond; Gan Shen) in the CASL research group, which is part of the Languages, Systems, and Data (LSD) Lab. Our work draws on the research traditions of programming languages, distributed systems, and software verification. Lately, we're especially excited about the potential of solver-aided tools for building trustworthy distributed systems. Our research has been supported by an NSF CAREER Award, a Google Faculty Research Award, and a gift from Amazon Web Services.

UC Santa Cruz has a strong and growing group of researchers who work in the areas of programming languages, systems, and databases, with myriad connections to security, formal methods, architecture, and beyond. Consider applying to our Ph.D. program and joining the LSD Lab!
